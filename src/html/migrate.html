What's "Migrate" vs "Convert" ?

Migrate simulates Suspended tabs like you might be used to. It's old-school.
Convert will un-Suspend your tabs, then tell your browser to immediately Discard them. This is the preferred option, native to the browser, but removes your "Suspended" placeholder page.
